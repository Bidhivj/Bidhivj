How does chaos affect entanglement in coupled systems? Why do we see traces of classical orbits in quantum states? What is the relationship between quantum physics and classical chaos? I work with open dynamical systems that display chaos in the classical limit—kicked rotors, cat maps, baker's maps—studying the correspondence between quantum channels and their classical counterparts through spectral properties and Husimi distributions.
